Hello, I'm Ying-Jia Lin. I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Artificial Intelligence, College of Intelligent Computing (CoIC), Chang Gung University. Before joining CoIC, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science, National Tsing Hua University under the supervision of my PhD advisor, Prof. Hung-Yu Kao. I received my PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Cheng Kung University in 2024. Before that, I was a master's and undergraduate student at National Yang-Ming University and Chang Gung University, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the following areas (but are not limited to):

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Information Retrieval for Large Language Models (LLM)
  • Medical Text Mining
  • Vision-Language Models
  • Sequence Tagging
